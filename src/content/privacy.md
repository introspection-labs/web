# Privacy Policy

Last updated: [Date]

At Dawn, we believe that your privacy is fundamental. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use Dawn, our speech-to-text application for macOS. We're committed to transparency and protecting your personal information.

## Introduction

Dawn is designed with privacy at its core. Our mission is to provide powerful speech-to-text capabilities while ensuring that your conversations, notes, and transcriptions remain private and secure. This policy outlines our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection.

### Our Commitment

We are committed to:
- Processing your audio locally whenever possible
- Minimizing data collection to only what's necessary
- Never selling your personal information
- Providing you with control over your data

## Information We Collect

Dawn is designed to collect minimal information necessary for the app to function.

### Audio Data

When you use Dawn to transcribe speech:
- Audio is processed locally on your device when possible
- If cloud processing is required for certain features, audio may be temporarily sent to our servers
- Audio data is not stored on our servers unless you explicitly choose to sync your transcriptions
- You can disable cloud processing entirely in settings

### Usage Information

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve Dawn:
- App version and macOS version
- Feature usage patterns (anonymized)
- Crash reports and error logs (no personal data included)

### Account Information

If you choose to create an account:
- Email address (for account management)
- Preferences and settings
- Synced transcriptions (if you enable cloud sync)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve Dawn's functionality.

### Core Functionality

- **Speech Recognition**: Processing your audio to generate text transcriptions
- **Feature Improvement**: Using anonymized data to enhance accuracy and performance
- **Support**: Responding to your questions and providing technical assistance

### What We Don't Do

- We do not sell your personal information
- We do not use your transcriptions for advertising
- We do not share your data with third parties except as necessary for core functionality
- We do not use your audio for training AI models without your explicit consent

## Data Storage and Security

Your data security is our priority.

### Local Storage

- Transcriptions are stored locally on your Mac by default
- Audio files are processed and deleted immediately after transcription (unless you choose to save them)
- All local data is encrypted using macOS's built-in security features

### Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you enable cloud sync:
- Your transcriptions are encrypted before transmission
- Data is stored in secure, encrypted databases
- You can disable cloud sync at any time
- You can delete all cloud-stored data from your account settings

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:
- End-to-end encryption for data in transit
- Encryption at rest for stored data
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls and authentication

### Data Retention

- Local transcriptions: Stored until you delete them
- Cloud transcriptions: Stored until you delete them or close your account
- Audio files: Deleted immediately after processing (unless saved)
- Account data: Retained while your account is active

## Your Rights

You have control over your data and how it's used.

### Access and Portability

- Access all your stored transcriptions
- Export your data in standard formats
- Request a copy of your personal information

### Deletion

- Delete individual transcriptions at any time
- Delete all your data from cloud storage
- Close your account and delete all associated data

### Privacy Controls

- Disable cloud processing entirely
- Opt out of anonymous usage statistics
- Control which features have network access
- Manage data sync preferences

### Transparency

- Request information about what data we have about you
- Understand how your data is being used
- Receive notifications about significant policy changes
